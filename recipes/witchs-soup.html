Witch's Soup

Description

A soup of questionable ingredients. Enjoy at your own risk.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Eye of newt
  • Toe of frog
  • Wool of bat
  • Tongue of dog
  • Adder's fork
  • Blind-worm's sting
  • Lizard's leg
  • Owlet's wing

Steps

  1. Start a fire beneath your cauldron
  2. Add water to the cauldron. Stock may be preferable for a more refined flavor profile.
  3. Once the water is bubbling, add all ingredients
  4. Cook until it looks like a "hell-broth"
