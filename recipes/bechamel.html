Bechamel

Description

One of the five mother sauces. Pretty easy stuff.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Spices, as preferred

Steps

  1. In a saucepan set to medium heat, melt the butter
  2. Once the butter is melted, slowly add the volumetric equivalent of flour, stirring as added
  3. Continue to stir the butter and flour together until homogenous; this is the roux
  4. Continue stirring the roux until it emits a light nutty smell (it should be a light golden color)
  5. Reduce the heat to low and slowly add milk, whisking as added
  6. The milk will evaporate as you stir. At this point, you can continue to add milk and stir to achieve your desired thickness.
  7. Once desired thickness is achieved, season with salt to taste. Season with additional spices as desired.
